Getting Started
Connect Your Wallet

Connect Your Wallet 🔗

Welcome friend! Let's get you connected to hx.finance in just a few clicks.

Which Wallets Work? 🦊

Good news - if you've used DeFi before, your wallet probably works! We support:

  • MetaMask 🦊 (Most popular - our top pick!)
  • WalletConnect 🔵 (Works with 100+ wallets)
  • Coinbase Wallet 💙 (Not the exchange, the wallet app)
  • Rainbow 🌈 (Beautiful and user-friendly)
  • Trust Wallet 🛡️ (Great mobile option)

Don't have a wallet yet? No worries! Get MetaMask here (opens in a new tab) - it takes 2 minutes.

Let's Connect! Step-by-Step 🚀

Step 1: Visit hx.finance 🌐

Head over to hx.finance (opens in a new tab). Bookmark it to avoid phishing sites!

Step 2: Click "Connect Wallet" 🔌

Look for the connect button in the top right corner. Can't miss it!

Step 3: Choose Your Wallet 💼

A menu will pop up with wallet options. Pick yours!

Pro tip: If using MetaMask on mobile, open hx.finance inside the MetaMask browser.

Step 4: Add Hyperliquid Network 🔧

First time on Hyperliquid? No problem! If the network isn't added yet, we'll help you add it automatically.

What you'll see:

  • A popup asking to add Hyperliquid network
  • Just click "Approve" - we handle the rest!

Network Details (for the curious):

Network Name: Hyperliquid
RPC URL: https://rpc.hyperliquid.xyz/evm
Chain ID: 999
Symbol: HYPE
Explorer: https://explorer.hyperliquid.xyz

Step 5: Approve Connection ✅

Your wallet will ask: "Connect to hx.finance?"

  • Check the URL is correct
  • Click "Connect" or "Approve"
  • That's it!

Step 6: You're In! 🎉

Success! You'll see your address in the top right, something like: 0x1234...5678

What you can do now:

  • View your balances
  • Start swapping
  • Provide liquidity
  • Earn points!

Troubleshooting 🔧

"Wallet won't connect" 😕

Try this:

  1. Refresh the page (Ctrl+R or Cmd+R)
  2. Clear your browser cache
  3. Try a different browser
  4. Make sure your wallet is unlocked

"Wrong network" 🔄

Easy fix:

  1. Click the network name in your wallet
  2. Select "Hyperliquid"
  3. If not there, add it manually with the details above

"Need HYPE for gas?" ⛽

You'll need a tiny bit of HYPE for transactions:

  • 0.1 HYPE is plenty to start
  • Gas fees are super cheap (usually < $0.01)
  • Check out how to get HYPE

Security First! 🔒

The Golden Rules:

  • NEVER share your seed phrase - Not even with "support"
  • Always check the URL - Only use https://hx.finance (opens in a new tab)
  • Look for the padlock 🔒 - In your browser bar
  • Bookmark the real site - Avoid phishing

Red Flags 🚩

Watch out for:

  • DMs asking for your seed phrase
  • "hx-finance.com" or similar fake URLs
  • Pop-ups asking for private keys
  • Too-good-to-be-true offers

Mobile Users 📱

For best mobile experience:

  1. Use your wallet's built-in browser
  2. Or use WalletConnect for seamless connection
  3. Everything works great on mobile!

You're All Set! 🎊

Connected successfully? Amazing! You're ready to:

  • Get HYPE for gas - You'll need this
  • Make your first swap - Start earning points
  • Join our Discord (opens in a new tab) - Say hi to the community!

Pro tip: The first connection is always the hardest. After this, your wallet will remember hx.finance! 🧠
