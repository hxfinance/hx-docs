## The Big Conversion 💰

At TGE (Token Generation Event):
- 33% of total $HX supply goes to point holders
- Your share = (Your points ÷ Total points) × 33% supply

**Example Math:**
```
Total points: 100,000,000
Your points: 50,000
Your share: 0.05%
33% supply: 330,000,000 $HX
You get: 165,000 $HX tokens! 🎉
```
